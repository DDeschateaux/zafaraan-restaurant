Our Story

Nestled in the prestigious Dubai Marina, Zafaraan redefines Middle Eastern dining through a contemporary lens. Our name, meaning "saffron" in Arabic, reflects our commitment to precious ingredients and golden experiences.

Under the guidance of Chef Rashid Al-Mansouri, trained in Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe, we blend centuries-old traditions with modern culinary techniques. Each dish tells a story of heritage, innovation, and the vibrant flavors of the Levant.

From our hand-selected spice blends to our locally-sourced seafood from the Gulf, every element is crafted with meticulous attention to detail.
